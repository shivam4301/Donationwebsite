Making it happen !!!

By 2050, solar power is expected to be the world’s largest source of electricity.

Solar Now sought to engage Canadians in the global shift to renewable energy by creating opportunities for people to see and experience solar power firsthand.

Through the program, solar electricity systems were installed on prominent public sites in communities across Western Canada—municipal buildings, schools, libraries, community centres, even electric vehicle charging stations.

With each installation, Solar Now aimed to demonstrate how communities can produce their own power while helping fight climate change by generating clean, renewable electricity from the sun.

Bringing Solar Power to Those in Need

At EvoTech Innovation, we believe in the power of solar energy to transform lives. Millions around the world still lack access to reliable electricity, impacting their daily lives and hindering community development. We are committed to changing that.

Our Mission

  • Empowering Communities: We provide solar panels and systems to communities without access to electricity, empowering them to improve their quality of life.
  • Sustainable Development: By harnessing the sun's energy, we promote sustainable development and reduce reliance on fossil fuels, contributing to a cleaner planet.
  • Education and Training: We offer education and training programs to ensure communities can maintain and benefit from their solar installations for years to come.

How You Can Help

  • Volunteer: Join us in our mission by volunteering your time and expertise to support our projects.
  • Spread the Word: Help us raise awareness about the importance of solar energy and its impact on communities.

Transforming Lives, One Panel at a Time Together, we can make a difference. Join us in bringing light and hope to those who need it most.
